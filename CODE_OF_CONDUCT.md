# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in AeroRamp Vision respectful, professional, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of differing experience levels and viewpoints.
- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its safety/privacy responsibilities.
- Report security-sensitive concerns privately rather than in public issues.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private credentials, passenger imagery, airport security details, or exploit instructions.
- Repeatedly making unsupported production, safety, compliance, or certification claims.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a ban from project spaces.
