# Data sources and licensing

## Included fixture

`sample-data/synthetic-ramp.mp4` is generated by `scripts/generate_synthetic_video.py`. It contains programmatically drawn shapes representing an aircraft, generic service vehicle, and anonymous person. It has no third-party footage and is intended only to exercise real decoding, tracking, geometry, evidence, persistence, API, and review workflows.

## Public demo video

`sample-data/dvids-age-public.mp4` is a small processed derivative from DVIDS video `838428`, `Aerospace Ground Equipment ensures aircraft are ready for flight`. The source is published by DVIDS / U.S. Air Force, credited on DVIDS to Senior Airman Daira Jackson / 386th Air Expeditionary Wing, and marked public domain subject to DVIDS public-use restrictions.

The preparation script is `scripts/download_public_dataset.py`. It streams the official DVIDS HLS rendition, selects a short demo window, resamples and resizes it for CPU-friendly local development, and writes `sample-data/dvids-age-public.json`.
